📱 Mobile Setup
1
Install Expo Go on your phone from the App Store or Play Store
2
Make sure your phone is on the same WiFi as this PC
3
Android: Open Expo Go → tap "Scan QR Code" and scan above
iPhone: Open Camera app → point at QR → tap the banner
4
Or enter this URL manually in Expo Go:
exp://192.168.100.9:8082

⚠️ If it keeps reloading, your phone and PC may be on different networks.
Try connecting your phone to the same WiFi as your PC.
